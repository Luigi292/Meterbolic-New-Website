General Questions

Metabolic health refers to how well your body processes and uses energy. Good metabolic health means your body efficiently converts food into energy without excessive fat storage or insulin resistance, reducing risk for chronic diseases like diabetes, heart disease, and Alzheimer's.

88% of American adults have degraded metabolic health, which is linked to most chronic diseases. Optimizing metabolic health can improve energy levels, mental clarity, longevity, and reduce healthcare costs by preventing disease before it starts.

Traditional medicine often waits until disease appears. Meterbolic focuses on prevention through advanced testing (like our exclusive Kraft Testing) that detects metabolic dysfunction up to 20 years before standard tests show issues, allowing for early intervention.

Testing Questions

While standard tests only measure glucose levels, Kraft Testing analyzes insulin response patterns through multiple blood draws during a glucose challenge. This reveals insulin resistance and metabolic dysfunction years before glucose levels become abnormal, when intervention is most effective.

Kraft Baseline: 15-minute test measuring fasting insulin (ideal for initial screening)
Full Kraft Test: 3-4 hour comprehensive analysis of insulin behavior during glucose challenge (provides complete metabolic profile)

  • Fast for 12 hours before testing (water is okay)
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours prior
  • Continue medications unless directed otherwise
  • Wear comfortable clothing with sleeve access
  • Bring a book or laptop as Full Kraft Test takes 3-4 hours

We recommend:

  • All adults: Baseline test by age 30
  • Annual testing: For maintenance monitoring
  • Every 3-6 months: When making lifestyle changes or reversing metabolic issues
  • Corporate programs: Typically annual testing with optional mid-year checkups

You'll receive:

  • Detailed digital report via secure portal within 3-5 business days
  • Personalized interpretation from our metabolic specialists
  • Actionable recommendations tailored to your results
  • Option for consultation to discuss findings
