OUR TESTS

How do we test for metabolic distress?

Kraft Baseline

The Kraft Baseline test measures fasting insulin, which reveals important insights into metabolic health. Insulin is crucial for regulating blood sugar levels, and its response patterns can serve as early indicators of chronic disease risk. The test detects hyperinsulinemia—a condition linked to diabetes, hypertension, and other health issues. Dr. Joseph Kraft referred to this as "occult diabetes."

Ideal For: Quick, comprehensive health snapshots and early detection of potential health risks.

Kraft Test

The Kraft Test offers a comprehensive analysis of insulin behavior through an Oral Glucose Tolerance Test (OGTT). During this test, insulin levels are measured after consuming a glucose drink while fasting, providing detailed insights over a three-hour period.

Ideal For: In-depth early detection of diabetes and comprehensive metabolic health analysis.
